Grilled Salmon Recipe

Ingredients

  • Salmon Fillet, approx. 3lbs with skin on side
  • Olive Oil
  • Your favorite seasonings

Instructions

  1. On a cutting board you can easily clean, slice salmon fillet into approx. 8oz pieces
  2. Preheat grill to 400 degrees
  3. Add a small amount of olive oil to each fillet
  4. Add you favorite seasonings to each fillet
  5. Grill salmon skin side down for 8 minutes and then flip for another 4 minutes

Nutrition Information

Content Amount per serving
Protein 50g
Sugar 0g
